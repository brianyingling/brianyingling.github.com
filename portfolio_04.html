The Internet Museum of Art

The Met. The Louvre. The British Museum. They all have brilliant and comprehensive art collections. Imagine if you could collect data on all your favorite paintings from history's finest artists.

In a central database, This project essentially saves data on various art pieces. Technologies used include PostgreSQL and Ruby on Rails.

Future iterations of this web app will include an API for data storage; possibly even search functionality to determine the location of various artworks.
