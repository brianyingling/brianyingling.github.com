TutorialTube

Imagine having to sift though the Internet just to find your favorite tutorials. With TutorialTube, all you need to do is save your favorite links with the click of a button!

TutorialTube is one of our earliest assignments demonstrating the power of the Model-View-Controller framework. Like all Web sites, TutorialTube was built using HTML and CSS. The back-end however, uses Sinatra, a Rails-light web framework. TutorialTube saves its data using PostgresSQL.
